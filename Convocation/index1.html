Event Schedule

"Welcome to the schedule for our Convocation Day! Join us as we celebrate achievements, honor graduates, and share memorable moments. Let’s make this day remarkable together!"

8:00 AM 1 Hours

Workshop Registration and Breakfast ☕

Start your special day with our Convocation Breakfast, a warm gathering to celebrate achievements and connect with fellow graduates, faculty, and guests. Enjoy a delightful spread of delicious breakfast items, freshly brewed coffee, and refreshing beverages. It's the perfect moment to share your excitement, exchange stories, and begin the day with a spirit of camaraderie before the official convocation ceremonies commence. Let’s toast to your hard-earned success and the journey ahead!

9:00 AM 1 Hours

Special Person Speech

In a world filled with voices, the words of this special person resonate deeply. Their valuable speech embodies wisdom, inspiration, and insight, encouraging us to reflect on our journeys and aspirations. With a unique perspective, they touch on themes of resilience, empathy, and the importance of community. Their message not only motivates but also fosters a sense of connection, reminding us of the power we hold to effect positive change. As they share their experiences and knowledge, listeners are left with a renewed sense of purpose and the courage to pursue their dreams.

10:00 AM 3 Hours

Honoring Graduates: Certificates and Medals

During the convocation ceremony, graduates are recognized for their hard work and dedication. The graduation certificate signifies the culmination of years of study and represents academic achievement. Graduates who excel academically may be awarded a gold medal, honoring their exceptional performance and commitment to excellence. These awards not only celebrate individual accomplishments but also inspire fellow students to strive for greatness. Each certificate and medal reflects the collective spirit of our academic community, emphasizing the importance of perseverance, knowledge, and growth. As graduates receive their honors, they embark on new journeys, carrying with them the pride of their achievements and the promise of future success.
